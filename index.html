Personal website

In case you don’t know me…

My name is Michel Voßkuhle. I recently finished a PhD in Physics at the École Normale Supérieure de Lyon under the supervision of A. Pumir and E. Lévêque. At the moment I am looking for new challenges.

About this site

On this website I provide information which I think someone entering my name in a search engine would be interested in. If you do not find what you are looking for, feel free to send me an email at <m.vosskuhle@gmail.com>.
